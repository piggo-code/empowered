Who we are

Empowered Women Rising Trust Association (EWRTA) is a non-political and non-religious organisation formed during the COVID-19 era to support women and girls through collective empowerment, economic collaboration, and social welfare initiatives.

What began as a small group of women intentionally supporting one another has grown into a community-driven movement committed to dignity, resilience, and sustainable impact.

Our Focus Areas

Driving sustainable change through targeted initiatives

Women Economic Empowerment

Building financial independence through skills training and entrepreneurship support.

Networking & Mentorship

Connecting women with leaders and mentors to foster growth and opportunity.

Girl Child Support

Advocating for education rights and providing resources for young girls.

Support for Young Mothers

Offering care, counseling, and essential resources for maternal health.

Community Outreach

Mobilizing resources and donations to uplift vulnerable community members.

Why We Exist

We believe that empowered women build strong families, and protected girls build strong futures. Through collaboration and compassion, we create spaces where women and girls can rise together.
