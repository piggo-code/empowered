At Empowered Women Rising Trust Association, our programs are designed to create lasting impact by equipping women with economic opportunities, fostering mentorship and leadership, and safeguarding the dignity of the girl child. Each initiative is strategically developed to strengthen communities, restore hope, and build a future where every woman and girl can rise with confidence and purpose.

Our Initiatives

What We Do

Each program addresses a critical need in our community, designed with input from the women and girls we serve.

Women Economic Empowerment

We support women-led businesses by creating platforms for networking, collaboration, and intentional support of products and services within our community. Through skills training, market access, and financial literacy, we help women build sustainable livelihoods.

Business Support Financial Literacy Market Access

Networking & Mentorship

Through meetings, discussions, and shared experiences, we foster mentorship and peer learning to help women grow personally and professionally. Our network connects emerging leaders with experienced mentors who guide their journey.

Mentorship Peer Learning Leadership

Girl Child Dignity Initiative

We prioritize the welfare and dignity of the girl child by providing sanitary supplies and essential items to vulnerable girls, helping them stay healthy, confident, and hopeful. No girl should miss school due to lack of basic necessities.

Sanitary Supplies Education Dignity

Support for Young Mothers

We support young mothers—particularly those who became pregnant while in school—through donations of diapers, sanitary supplies, and emotional encouragement, aiming to restore dignity and promote positive life outcomes for both mother and child.

Young Mothers Support Restoration
